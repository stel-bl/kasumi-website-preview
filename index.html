Features

Overlays. Lots of overlays.

With the power of source and runtime resource overlays, we replace your favorite ROM's most viewed parts with our own designs made around the design concept found in BanG Dream GBP.

Plug-and-Play: ✓

With how generic the overlays were crafted, along with the most straightforward documentation you can ever find regarding them, you can easily plug and play our sources on whatever ROM you want!*


* Compatibility depends on the base ROM's developer(s), consult your build maintainer for more info.
** As a result of this feature, the features provided in your build might be different than the features present on the build of Kasumi for another device.
